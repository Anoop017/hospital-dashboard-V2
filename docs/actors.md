ACTORS
<====>

There are three main actors,
1. Doctor
2. Patient
3. Admin(Super User)


For DOCTORS
<---------->
1. View Appointments from patients
2. Edit the Available timings
3. Update the upcoming timings and availability
4. Approve or reject the appointment timings
5. Edit the appointment timings / send request to admin for confirmation
6. The doctor can update or delete the details or health condition of his patients after appointments
7. The doctor can add or remove documents regarding the health condition of a patient

- So once the doctor approves the appointment, the patient will receive a notification
- The patient will receive a notification if Doctor rejects it
- The patient will receive a notification if doctor request a different time
- The patient will receive a notification if the doctor updates or modify the documents regarding the health status of patients.


For Patients
<----------->
1. View available doctors
2. Book appointments
3. Edit appointment timings
4. change username and password
5. View and share details of patients health


FOR ADMINS
<--------->
1. Super User
2. Can update, edit, delete or create anything
3. Can also edit the username and password of doctors and patients
4. View and update patients health reports
5. Can send notification regarding updates to both patient and doctors
